# Educational / Explainer

Use when the footage explains an idea, concept, framework, or lesson.

Common structure:
THESIS -> CONTEXT -> EXPLANATION -> EXAMPLE -> TAKEAWAY

Prioritize:
- clear thesis;
- useful context;
- concise explanation;
- concrete example;
- memorable takeaway.

Cut:
- repeated definitions;
- filler-heavy restatements;
- examples that do not clarify;
- tangents that obscure the lesson.

Common mistakes:
- opening with too much background;
- removing the example and leaving the idea abstract;
- over-tightening thoughtful pauses.

Target runtime guidance:
- preserve enough length for the viewer to understand and retain the idea.

Do not force all beats if the material does not support them.
