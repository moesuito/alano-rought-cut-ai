# Podcast Excerpt

Use when the source is a long-form conversation and the goal is a focused clip.

Common structure:
CONTEXT -> STRONG OPINION / STORY -> CLARIFICATION -> PAYOFF

Prioritize:
- enough context to understand the topic;
- strong opinion, story, or tension;
- clarifying turn that sharpens the point;
- payoff line or memorable ending.

Cut:
- preamble;
- side jokes that do not serve the excerpt;
- repeated agreement;
- long detours before the point;
- weak endings after the payoff.

Common mistakes:
- cutting too much context;
- ending after the energy has already dropped;
- ignoring speaker handoffs and reactions.

Target runtime guidance:
- usually 60-180s.

Do not force all beats if the material does not support them.
