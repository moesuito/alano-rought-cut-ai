# Documentary / Narrative

Use when the material builds a story, argument, character arc, or evidence-driven narrative.

Common structure:
THESIS -> EVIDENCE -> TENSION -> RESOLUTION -> CONCLUSION

Prioritize:
- thesis or central question;
- evidence moments;
- tension, contradiction, or stakes;
- emotional truth;
- resolution or thoughtful closing line.

Cut:
- repeated exposition;
- evidence that does not support the arc;
- low-energy tangents;
- overly neat transitions that weaken authenticity.

Common mistakes:
- forcing a sales-like structure;
- removing quiet moments that carry meaning;
- resolving tension too early.

Target runtime guidance:
- prioritize coherence and emotional continuity over aggressive shortening.

Do not force all beats if the material does not support them.
